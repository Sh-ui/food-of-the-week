## Week foundation (draft)

### Big move early week
- Make simple chicken stock: roast chicken parts hard, cover with water, simmer low. This becomes the liquid for multiple meals (fish cakes, lentils, beans).

### Dinners

#### Lentil curry + cabbage + sticky rice
- Curry base: onions + carrots + ginger + garlic, then lentils
- Curry flavor: Japanese curry cubes + chicken stock
- Rice: Japanese sticky rice
- Side: crisp shredded cabbage dressed with the last of the Japanese dressing

#### Fish cakes + spaghetti squash + slaw
- Fish cakes: flaked fish + egg + panko + green onion
- Use chicken stock instead of water where it helps (fish cake mix)
- Spaghetti squash: strands + olive oil + garlic + salt + pepper
- Slaw: either bagged chopped cabbage kit or improvise from remaining cabbage

#### Pad kra pao (ground beef)
- Ground beef + red onion + ginger + garlic + collards
- Served over rice

#### Chicken tacos (corn tortillas)
- Protein: cooked chicken breast, cumin + paprika (skipping salt since I'm using frozen marinated chicken that is already salty)
- Onions + green pepper saute
- Tortillas: homemade corn tortillas
- Add: cilantro + whatever fridge accompaniments (salsa, yogurt, etc.)
- Sides: thawed refried beans + rice

### Lunches / extras (since not working this week)

#### Freezer ragu + meatballs pasta lunch
- Ragu boosted with cooked onion
- Frozen meatballs
- Pantry pasta
- Finish: shredded Parmesan
- Side: steamed carrots (optional)

#### Brunch hash: beans + bacon + collards + hashbrowns + eggs
- Beans as base (pinto or white)
- Bacon for flavor
- Collards folded in
- Ready-to-cook hashbrowns
- Eggs on top

#### Wrap lunches
- Spinach herb wraps + hummus + olives + pickled onions
- Add: turkey + ham + cucumber
