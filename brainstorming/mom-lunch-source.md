# Mom's Lunch — Source Material (nutritionist handout)

Source for the `/lunch` page lunch pool. Mom received this from her nutritionist.
Two pages: a carb-serving reference and a low-carb small-meal/snack idea list.
The lunch pool (`src/data/lunch-pool.json`, built by `scripts/build-lunch-pool.mjs`)
is seeded from page 2, with carb estimates derived from page 1, plus fitting
lunch-buddy items and a small web expansion under the same principles.

## Page 1 — Sample Carb Servings (1 carb serving ≈ 15 g carbs unless noted)

**Starch / Grains** (15 g each): 1 slice/oz bread · ½ English muffin · ½ mini bagel
(¼ regular) · ½ hot dog/hamburger bun · ¾ C unsweetened plain cereal (Cheerios,
Rice Krispies, Cornflakes) · 4" pancake/waffle · ⅓ C cooked pasta/rice/quinoa/couscous
· ½ C cooked grits/oats/oatmeal · 3–5 whole-wheat crackers · 3 C popcorn · 6" corn or
4" flour tortilla.

**Starchy Vegetables** (15 g each): ⅓ C cassava/plantain · ½ C mashed potato or ½ baked
· ½ C corn or 1 cob · ½ C green peas · ½ C parsnips · ¾ C pumpkin · 1 C winter squash.
Beans are a starchy protein: ½ C lentils/kidney/lima/navy/pinto.

**Fruit** (15 g each): 1 small fruit (4 oz) · ½ C canned (own juice) · ¼ C or 2 Tbsp
dried · 17 small grapes (½ cup) · ½ C juice · 1¼ C strawberries/melon · 1 Tbsp jelly/jam
· 1 C blackberries/raspberries · ½ medium banana or mango.

**Milk / Dairy** (12 g each): 1 C milk (all types) · 1 C plain yogurt · 1 C plain/vanilla
soy milk.

**Sweets & Desserts** (15 g each): 2" square cake (unfrosted) · 2 small cookies · ½ C ice
cream/frozen yogurt · ¼ C sherbet/sorbet · 1 Tbsp syrup/honey · 2 Tbsp light syrup.
(1/6 of 8" pie = 45 g · 1 muffin 4 oz = 60 g.)

**Combination Foods** (30 g = 2 servings): 1 C casseroles/mixed foods (spaghetti, mac 'n
cheese).

> These are general guidelines. **Always check the food label** — manufacturers may add
> ingredients that change total carbohydrate.
