**Core Produce (buy once, use all week):**
- Bell peppers (2-3)
- Zucchini (2-3)
- Maybe one bunch cilantro

**Meal 1: Ground Bison** (Prep&Heat)
Sheet-pan: bison + carrots + bell peppers + red onion. Roast together, finish with garlic.

**Meal 2: Cod** (Prep&Heat)
One-pan: cod + zucchini + tomatoes. Quick braise with garlic/onion base.

**Meal 3: Italian Sausage** (Prep&Heat)
Sheet-pan: sausage + bell peppers + zucchini + red onion. Roast, serve over rice.

**Meal 4: Chicken Breast** (Prep&Heat+)
Skillet: chicken + carrots + green beans. Pan-sear chicken, quick veg saut√©, finish with garlic.

**Meal 5: Salmon** (Prep&Heat)
Sheet-pan: salmon + zucchini + carrots. Roast together, finish with cilantro.

**Reuse pattern:**
- Carrots: meals 1, 4, 5
- Bell peppers: meals 1, 3
- Zucchini: meals 2, 3, 5
- Red onion: meals 1, 3
- Garlic: all meals
- Cabbage/green beans: meal 4
