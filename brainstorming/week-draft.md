# Week Draft v3 - March 2-8

## Grocery Strike (~$7-10)
- 1lb ground beef
- 1 pack pitas or naan
- 1 head cabbage

## The Week (5 cooking events → 7 dinners)

### Monday - One Pot Penne (TODAY)
One pot: penne, frozen chicken meatballs, frozen ragu, diced bell pepper, diced onion.
Parm shaved on the pasta.

Soak pinto beans tonight for later in the week.

### Tuesday - Chicken Shawarma Pitas
The 2 marinated chicken breasts - salty/brined in thyme, garlic, bouillon, white wine.
Cook them (sous vide for texture control, or roast), slice thin. The salt works FOR this application.

- Bought pitas/naan, warmed
- Tzatziki: greek yogurt + lemon juice + garlic + salt
- Warm spiced chickpeas: canned garbanzos, cumin, paprika, garlic, lemon squeeze. Smash some, leave some whole.
- Feta crumbles
- Shredded cabbage
- Pickled red onions

### Wednesday - Ground Beef Chili
Brown 1lb ground beef with diced onion and garlic. Add canned chopped tomatoes, canned whole tomatoes (crush by hand), cooked pinto beans (from Monday soak → Tuesday simmer), cumin, paprika, chili, whatever spices.
Let it cook low and slow - you're home, let it do its thing on the stove.

Serve over Japanese white rice. Top with: shredded cabbage, greek yogurt, pickled red onions, cherry pepper relish for heat.

Makes enough for Thursday leftovers.

### Thursday - Chili Leftovers
Reheat chili. Serve over rice again, or with the tostada shells from the pantry if they're still around.
Same toppings. Different vehicle.

Zero cooking. Coast day before the peak.

### Friday - Tenderloin + Dutch Babies (THE PEAK)
Sous vide the whole tenderloin. Sear hard. Deglaze sear pan with bag juices → quick gravy.

Dutch babies: flour, eggs, butter, heavy cream. Puffs up in the oven - spectacle, cheap, satisfying.
Seared cabbage wedges: thick wedges, char in the beef pan after searing. Pick up the fond.
Frozen peas: warmed with butter.

Cook the WHOLE tenderloin. Eat steaks tonight. Reserve sliced meat for tomorrow.

### Saturday - Steak Sandwiches (COAST DAY)
Cold sliced tenderloin on toasted sourdough.
Cherry pepper relish. Pickled red onions. American cheese melted under broiler.
Assembly, not cooking. 15 minutes.

### Sunday - Fridge Clean-Out
Whatever's left. Options:
- Remaining chili if any + rice
- Red lentil dal (red lentils + leftover canned tomatoes + onion + garlic, 25 min) over rice
- Refried beans + rice from freezer with whatever toppings remain
- Collard greens warmed up as a side to any of the above
