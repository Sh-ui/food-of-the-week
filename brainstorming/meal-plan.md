# Weekly Meal Plan

## Meal 1: One Pot Pasta
**Protein:** Ground pork  
**Ingredients:** Brown udon rice noodles, tomato paste, San Marzano tomatoes, frozen mixed mushrooms, frozen three pepper blend  
**Description:** One pot pasta with pork and vegetables

## Meal 2: Broiled Steak Dinner
**Protein:** Flat iron steak  
**Ingredients:** White sweet potatoes, green cabbage, red onion, Italian dressing marinade (at home)  
**Description:** Marinated broiled steak with roasted vegetables

## Meal 3: Stir Fry
**Protein:** Sirloin flap steak  
**Ingredients:** Purple asparagus, bok choy microgreens, shallot, brown rice (at home)  
**Description:** Stir fried steak and asparagus with microgreens

## Meal 4: Roasted Chicken
**Protein:** Whole chicken  
**Ingredients:** Collard greens, golden beets, red beets, pomegranate  
**Description:** Roasted chicken with braised greens, beets, and pomegranate garnish
